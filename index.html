The Process

From application to auditable receipt

01
District Applies
Registered district offices submit funding applications with purpose and budget on-chain.
02
AI Ranks Priority
AI evaluates urgency, population impact, and risk. Scores stored permanently on-chain.
03
Head Office Decides
Approval requires a mandatory reason stored immutably. Every decision is public and timestamped.
04
Funds Released
ETH transfers directly to district wallet. Receipt recorded on-chain. Auditable forever by anyone.
